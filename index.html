Get To Know Me

My name is Austin Witherow. I was born and raised in Mechanicsville, Virginia where most of my life was spent at the local swimming pool during the summer, or competitively swimming year around. When I was not in the pool, I was always reading books, making art or tinkering around with the computer. The competitive swimming career lead me to a scholarship Division One career at George Mason University where I was the captain of the team for 3 of my 4 years, and a recipient of over 14 medals through hard work and commitment to success.

Since then, I've been working on developing my Professional Skillset which focus on Web and Application Development and Yoga, Mindfulness and Meditation. In my spare time, I enjoy gardening, reading, hiking, playing guitar, swimming, acrobatics, eastern martial arts, and more!
