Class Style: A rEvolutionary blend of Qi Gong, Hatha Yoga, Kundalini Yoga with Breathwork and Meditation.

This practice is one I have developed over the last 10 years of exploration, learning and certification in Yoga and experience with Qi Gong/Tai Chi. Each class includes a physical practice that focus on joint and spinal health, flexibility and alignment, athletic skill progression, whole body aesthetics, ending with restorative poses accompanied with Reiki Adjustment Therapy in Savasana. Following the physical practice, we practice a simple and powerful Yoga Nidra and end with Deep Meditation. The entire practice is calming yet energizing, relaxing yet stimulating.
