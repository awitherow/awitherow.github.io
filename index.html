Get To Know Me

My name is Austin Witherow. I was born and raised in Mechanicsville, Virginia where most of my life was spent at the local swimming pool during the summer, or competitively swimming year around. When I was not in the pool, I was always reading books, making art or tinkering around with the computer. The competitive swimming career lead me to a scholarship Division One career at George Mason University where I was the captain of the team for 3 of my 4 years, and a recipient of over 14 medals through hard work and commitment to success.

Technical Proficiencies

Since graduating with my degree in Applied Information Technology with Business and Web Development concentrations, I have worked as a graphic designer and web developer in America and Germany with over 5 years of experience. I am now working with individuals and small businesses to bring quality into the world at minimal costs. Yoga and Spirituality

At the age of 18 upon getting accepted into George Mason on my swimming scholarship, I began looking up competitive edges so that I could make the best of that upcoming career. Everywhere I read, meditation was key. I discovered a wonderful site, Advanced Yoga Practices and began immediately integrating their twice daily practices. As these practices began to scrub away deep karmic conditioning and truly transform my life, my bhakti steadily lead me to seek Truth in everything. My passion is effective practice for gradual and beautiful spiritual unfoldment and through my learning and understanding of Yoga, it is my main desire in life to share this with the world so that everyone can reach deeper levels of fulfillment, transcend suffering and find immortal happiness.
